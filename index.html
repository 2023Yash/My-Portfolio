About Me

I was born at a very young age
And that's where the achievvments end for now.

I don't really have a single hobby. I just have a habit of finding something interesting and wondering, "How does that work?" That question has led me into programming, art, Linux, web development, Python, typography, Minecraft engineering, and more side projects than I'd like to admit.

I enjoy creating things, whether that's a website, a painting, a small Python script, or something that started as "This should only take twenty minutes." (It almost never does.)

Things I Make

  • 🌐 Websites that people actually enjoy using.
  • 🎨 Art that began as "just a quick sketch."
  • 🐍 Python projects that solve everyday problems.
  • ⚙️ Minecraft contraptions that definitely weren't necessary.

Factory Settings

  • 🐧 Uses Linux... voluntarily.
  • 🧩 Solves Rubik's Cubes for fun.
  • 🎵 Usually listening to Eminem.

I don't really separate programming from art. To me they're the same thing with different tools. One uses a keyboard, the other uses a pencil, but both start with a blank canvas and usually end with "...one more tiny change."

I'm still learning, still experimenting, and still finding new rabbit holes to disappear into. I probably won't know everything tomorrow. But I'll almost certainly have another project.
