About Me

Hi! I'm Yash Naudiyal, a student, programmer, digital artist, Linux enthusiast, and professional creator of projects that started with "this might be fun." I was born at a very young age, which, statistically speaking, was an excellent time to be born. I enjoy building websites, writing Python programs, experimenting with Linux, and figuring out how things work under the hood. When I'm not coding, I'm usually drawing, learning something new, or spending an unreasonable amount of time engineering large-scale Minecraft farms because "good enough" has never sounded particularly convincing. I enjoy learning by building real projects instead of just reading about them. Every project teaches me something new, whether it's web development, cryptography, automation, or simply discovering a better way to solve a problem. Outside of technology, I enjoy digital art and design, combining creativity with logic to build things that are both functional and visually appealing. 'm always looking for the next challenge because staying comfortable is overrated, and computers are surprisingly good at reminding you that you don't know everything.
